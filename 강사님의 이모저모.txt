            -----------------------샘플 질문-------------------------
            To make a strong impression and gather meaningful insights during your interview, here are some thoughtful questions that will demonstrate your interest in the company, team dynamics, and technical challenges. These questions are broadly applicable and can help you assess if the company is a good fit, regardless of the specific technologies they use.

            1. **Questions about the Company and Team Culture**
            - *"How would you describe the team dynamics here? Do teams typically work in a collaborative, cross-functional manner?"*
            - *"What does success look like for someone in this role in the first 90 days? After one year?"*
            - *"How does the company support professional development, learning, and skill-building for developers?"*
            - *"Can you describe the company's approach to work-life balance, especially during tight deadlines or high-priority projects?"*

            2. **Questions on Project Management and Workflow**
            - *"How do you handle project prioritization and time management? Are there any specific tools or methodologies you rely on, such as Agile, Scrum, or Kanban?"*
            - *"How often do code reviews happen, and who typically reviews the code? What are the team's expectations around code quality?"*
            - *"What is the general process when a team encounters a technical roadblock or needs to pivot due to unforeseen issues?"*

            3. **Questions on Technical Challenges and Problem-Solving**
            - *"What is one of the most technically challenging projects the team has worked on recently? How did you address and overcome the difficulties?"*
            - *"Are there any large-scale issues the engineering team is actively working on solving or optimizing?"*
            - *"How does the company approach technical debt? Are there regular reviews or dedicated time to refactor and improve existing code?"*

            4. **Questions about Growth and Innovation**
            - *"How does the company encourage and manage innovation within the engineering team? Are there opportunities for developers to suggest or lead new initiatives?"*
            - *"What are some of the goals for the development team in the coming year? How does this role align with those goals?"*
            - *"Is the team currently exploring any emerging technologies or new tools that could significantly impact future projects?"*

            5. **Questions on Collaboration and Cross-Functional Interaction**
            - *"How do engineers typically collaborate with other departments, such as design, product, or QA? What does that process look like?"*
            - *"Are there regular touchpoints between the development team and business units to ensure alignment on goals and expectations?"*

            6. **Questions on Testing, Security, and Quality Assurance**
            - *"What is the team's approach to testing and quality assurance? Are there dedicated QA engineers, or is testing a shared responsibility?"*
            - *"How does the company ensure data security, and what are the key security practices for code and infrastructure?"*
            - *"Is there a particular emphasis on specific testing methodologies, such as unit testing, integration testing, or end-to-end testing?"*

            7. **Questions on Onboarding and Mentorship**
            - *"How does the company handle onboarding for new developers? Is there a structured program in place?"*
            - *"Does the team have any mentorship opportunities for junior developers or newer team members?"*

            8. **Questions about the Company’s Technical Stack and Long-Term Vision**
            - *"What does the current tech stack look like, and how often does the team reassess or update it?"*
            - *"How does the engineering team balance adopting new technologies with maintaining stable production environments?"*

            9. **Questions on the Interviewer’s Personal Experience**
            - *"What do you personally find most rewarding about working here, and what has kept you with the company?"*
            - *"What has been one of your most memorable projects, and what did you learn from it?"*

            10. **Closing Question**
                - *"Is there anything about my background or experience that I could clarify further to ensure I'm a strong match for this role?"*

            These questions should help you better understand the company and the team’s culture, while also showing that you’re proactive, thoughtful, and keen to integrate well into their environment.

            Certainly! Here’s a set of practical, no-nonsense questions that can help you get a clearer picture of the working conditions, company expectations, and whether this role aligns with your personal goals and lifestyle. While these questions are more direct, they can still be phrased diplomatically to show that you're genuinely interested in understanding the reality of the role.

            1. **Compensation and Benefits**
            - *"Could you provide more detail on the salary range for this role and any performance-based incentives?"*
            - *"How often do salary reviews or promotions occur, and what criteria are they based on?"*
            - *"What are the additional benefits or perks offered by the company beyond health insurance and paid time off?"*

            2. **Location and Work Arrangements**
            - *"Where will I primarily be located, and is there an expectation to work from specific offices or client sites?"*
            - *"If I’m stationed at a location other than the main branch, how long would that assignment typically last?"*
            - *"Is there flexibility in working from home, or is remote work discouraged here? If hybrid, how is that structured?"*

            3. **Travel and Relocation Expectations**
            - *"Are there frequent travel requirements for this role, and if so, how often?"*
            - *"Is there any expectation for relocation, either now or potentially in the future as the team grows or projects shift?"*
            - *"If relocation is required, does the company assist with relocation expenses, and how does it support employees in transitioning smoothly?"*

            4. **Work Hours and Flexibility**
            - *"What are the typical working hours for this role? Are there often expectations to work overtime or during weekends?"*
            - *"How does the company handle work-life balance, particularly during peak periods or project crunch times?"*
            - *"Is there any flexibility in terms of work hours if I need to adjust for personal obligations occasionally?"*

            5. **Project Assignment and Duration**
            - *"How are projects typically assigned to team members? Is it based on individual strengths, or are team members expected to cover a variety of projects?"*
            - *"Can you give me an idea of the average project length and how often developers transition between projects?"*
            - *"Is there a set timeline for specific projects, or could I be reassigned if other priorities come up?"*

            6. **Career Growth and Development**
            - *"How does the company support career progression, and what are the realistic timelines for advancement?"*
            - *"Does the company prioritize promoting from within, or are external candidates often brought in for higher-level positions?"*
            - *"If I wanted to transition into a different department or a more specialized role in the future, how easy is it to do so within the company?"*

            7. **Job Security and Performance Expectations**
            - *"What are the company's expectations for someone in this role within the first six months, and how is performance evaluated?"*
            - *"How does the company handle underperformance or mismatches between role expectations and employee output?"*
            - *"Is this position tied to specific projects or funding sources, and would it be impacted if project priorities or funding changed?"*

            8. **Team Dynamics and Managerial Style**
            - *"How would you describe the management style here? Is it more hands-on, or is there a preference for autonomous work?"*
            - *"How are conflicts or disagreements within the team typically handled?"*
            - *"Does the team have any regular activities or meetings outside of work hours, and is there an expectation to attend?"*

            9. **Long-Term Vision and Stability**
            - *"What are some of the company’s long-term goals, and how does this team contribute to them?"*
            - *"Has the company experienced major reorganizations or restructuring in recent years, and if so, how have those changes impacted teams?"*
            - *"What are the main factors driving growth for the company, and do you anticipate any major shifts in focus or priorities soon?"*

            10. **Team Turnover and Satisfaction**
                - *"Could you give me an idea of the team turnover rate? Has the company seen a lot of changes in the team composition recently?"*
                - *"What are some of the main reasons people choose to stay with the company long-term, and what challenges have been reported in employee satisfaction?"*
                - *"Is there anything about this role or the team that you believe could be challenging for new hires?"*

            These questions can help you gain insight into aspects of the job that may not come up in a typical interview but are important for your decision-making. Adjust the phrasing if needed to suit the tone of your conversation, as this direct approach may be better suited for later interview stages or when speaking with a hiring manager.

            1. **회사와 팀 문화에 관한 질문**
            - *"여기 팀의 다이나믹스는 어떤가요? 팀 간 협업이나 교차 기능적 협력이 활발한 편인가요?"*
            - *"이 역할에서 첫 90일 동안 또는 1년 후에 성공적인 모습은 어떤 것인가요?"*

            2. **프로젝트 관리와 워크플로우에 관한 질문**
            - *"프로젝트 우선순위 설정과 시간 관리는 어떻게 이루어지나요? Agile, Scrum, Kanban 같은 특정 툴이나 방법론을 사용하는지 궁금합니다."*
            - *"코드 리뷰는 얼마나 자주 이루어지며, 주로 누가 리뷰를 담당하나요? 코드 품질에 대한 팀의 기대치는 어떤가요?"*
            - *"기술적 장애물에 직면하거나 예기치 못한 이유로 방향을 전환해야 할 때 일반적인 프로세스는 어떻게 되나요?"*

            3. **기술적 과제와 문제 해결에 관한 질문**
            - *"최근 팀이 작업한 가장 기술적으로 도전적인 프로젝트는 무엇이었나요? 문제를 해결하거나 극복하기 위해 어떤 접근을 했나요?"*
            - *"현재 엔지니어링 팀이 해결하거나 최적화하려고 적극적으로 작업하고 있는 대규모 문제는 있나요?"*
            - *"기술적 부채에 대해 회사는 어떤 접근 방식을 가지고 있나요? 리팩터링이나 기존 코드 개선을 위해 정기적으로 리뷰가 이루어지나요?"*

            4. **성장과 혁신에 관한 질문**
            - *"엔지니어링 팀 내에서 혁신을 장려하고 관리하기 위해 회사는 어떤 시스템을 운영하나요? 새로운 아이디어를 제안하거나 주도할 기회가 있나요?"*
            - *"향후 1년간 개발팀의 목표는 무엇인가요? 이 역할이 그 목표와 어떻게 일치하는지 궁금합니다."*
            - *"팀이 현재 프로젝트에 큰 영향을 미칠 수 있는 새로운 기술이나 도구를 탐구하고 있나요?"*

            5. **협업 및 교차 기능 상호작용에 관한 질문**
            - *"개발자들이 디자인, 제품, QA와 협력할 때 일반적으로 어떤 방식으로 협업이 이루어지나요?"*
            - *"개발팀과 다른 부서 간의 목표와 기대가 일치하도록 정기적으로 만나서 논의하는 자리가 있나요?"*

            6. **테스트, 보안, 품질 보증에 관한 질문**
            - *"팀의 테스트와 품질 보증 접근 방식은 어떤가요? 전담 QA 엔지니어가 있나요, 아니면 테스트가 공동 책임인가요?"*
            - *"회사는 데이터 보안을 어떻게 관리하며, 코드와 인프라의 보안을 위해 어떤 주요 보안 방식을 사용하는지 궁금합니다."*
            - *"단위 테스트, 통합 테스트, 종단 간 테스트 같은 특정 테스트 방법론에 대해 중점을 두고 있나요?"*

            7. **온보딩 및 멘토링에 관한 질문**
            - *"신규 개발자 온보딩은 어떻게 진행되나요? 구조화된 프로그램이 있나요?"*
            - *"주니어 개발자나 새 팀원들을 위한 멘토링 기회가 마련되어 있나요?"*

            8. **기술 스택 및 장기적 비전에 관한 질문**
            - *"현재 기술 스택은 어떤 모습이며, 팀은 얼마나 자주 이를 재평가하거나 업데이트하나요?"*
            - *"새로운 기술을 도입할 때 안정적인 프로덕션 환경을 유지하는 것과 어떻게 균형을 맞추나요?"*

            9. **인터뷰어의 개인 경험에 관한 질문**

            - *"가끔 개인적인 사정으로 근무 시간을 조정해야 할 때 어느 정도 유연성을 허용하나요?"*

            - *"평균적인 프로젝트 길이는 어느 정도이며, 팀원들은 얼마나 자주 프로젝트를 변경하나요?"*
